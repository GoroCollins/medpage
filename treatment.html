Reliability of Core Measures

  • History: At least two of:Fever, Reduced level of consciousness, Stiff neck, Bulging fontanelle, Convulsions, Vomiting
  • Examination:Fever, Coma or AVPU < V, inability to drink / feed, Stiff neck, Positive Kernig's sign, Bulging fontanelle
  • Plan of Care:Admit, Lumbar Puncture +/- Head CT Scan, Haemogram, Urea, Electrolytes and Creatinine
  • Management:Appropriate antibiotics [Ceftriaxone]
  • Analgesic/Antipyretic
